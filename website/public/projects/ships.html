Ships!

Summary

This game is a 2-4 player party game in which players control a pirate ship and must shoot cannons to destroy other ships. The niche to the game is that to reload the cannonballs, the player must reload by spinning a joystick in rotation, wish increasing amounts of loaded ammo for consecutive spinning. A player wins when they are the last boat standing.

This game has an alternate version where two people control the same boat, one person steers and the other shoots and reloads.
